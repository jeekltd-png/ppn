### Recommended Format
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution:** 1280x720 (720p) or 1920x1080 (1080p)
- **Bitrate:** 2-4 Mbps
- **File Size:** Under 50MB per video

### Naming Convention
- `sermon-week1.mp4`
- `sermon-week2.mp4`
- `conference-birmingham-2025.mp4`
- `prayer-session-january.mp4`

### Alternative: Use YouTube/Vimeo
For better performance and free hosting, consider uploading to:
- **YouTube:** https://youtube.com
- **Vimeo:** https://vimeo.com

Then embed the videos in the website instead of hosting them directly.
